Project Name: WorldSurf

Project Description

This month I will design a series of interfaces intended for specific use which will be referred to as the “main intent” of the site or application. There will be several usability tests created this month that will reveal where users click when navigating my interfaces, while trying to accomplish the main intent which will be broken down into tasks/goals. I will write the task or goal of each sequence of wireframes as the “instructions” on my Usability Tests. The task based interactions I design for the users of the site will support the Main Intent, as well as reflect the “business and user goals” I define early in the “Discovery” phase of the product development. The Usability tests and their results will be stored at the bottom of this landing page in separate hyperlinks where the information to each will be accessible to the readership of this site. Thank you for your time and help. -Chad
